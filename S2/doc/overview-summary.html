Muty: a distributed mutual-exclusion lock

Version: 1.0.0

Authors: Martin Garcia (marti.garcia@est.fib.upc.edu), Matias Amandola.

Muty is a distributed mutual-exclusion lock. The lock use a multicast strategy and work in an asynchronous network where we do not have access to a synchronized clock. Implemented in three versions: the deadlock prone, the unfair, and the Lamport clocked. Based in the Ricart and Agrawala mutual exclusion algorithm.
